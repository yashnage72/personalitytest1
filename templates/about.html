About the App

This Personality Prediction App consists of a questionnaire which uses Big Five model to precit a person's personality and suggest music related to their personality. The Big Five Traits are openness , extraversion, agreeableness, neuroticism and conscientiousness. The app will also provide a detailed result after the quiz about where the user ranks based on big five traits.
